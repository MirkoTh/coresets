Information on tasks and purpose of the study

Today, you are carrying out two different tasks with basic objects, arrows of different lengths and orientations.
You are slipping into the role of a city surveyor, Mira, who examines various of these basic objects.



The city's road network is relatively special.
So, the following are going to be your tasks:
  • Category Learning: Mira only likes some of the arrows, because they are useful for the city's road network.
    Learn to categorize the arrows into those, which Mira likes, and those, which Mira dislikes.
  • Memory: Remember whether you have already seen a certain arrow before or not

The purpose of the study is to gain knowledge about how people mentally represent two-dimensional information.
On the next page you will get detailed instructions about the specific combination of tasks you are going to carry out.

Instructions

Your session today will be split up into two tasks:

  1. Mira's preferences:On every trial you are presented with an arrow.
    As the city's roadwork is relatively special, Mira only likes certain arrows, but not others.
    Your task is to learn which objects Mira likes and which ones not.
    If you think, Mira likes the object, press the 'j' button on the keyboard.
    If you think, Mira does not like the object, press the 'f' button on the keyboard.
    You will get help via feedback on every trial.
    --> Takes approx. 15 min

  2. Recognition:You are going to see the arrows presented in 'Mira's preferences' together with new arrows.
    Your task is to say if you have seen the respective arrow presented on a given trial or not as accurately and quickly as possible.
    If the object has been presented before, press the 'j' button.
    If the object has not been presented before, press the 'f' button.
    --> Takes approx. 15 min

  3. Mira's preferences reloaded:The task is similar to 'Mira's preferences'.
    You again have to say if Mira likes the object or not. However, this time, you will not get any feedback to sharpen your understanding of Mira's preferences.
    Please use again the same response buttons as in the first part:
    If you think, Mira likes the object, press the 'j' button on the keyboard.
    If you think, Mira does not like the object, press the 'f' button on the keyboard.
    --> Takes approx. 10 min
